School Internal Result

School Admission Result 2023 is going to be published on 13 December. DSHE has announced the Non-Government School Lottery Result merit list. The digital lottery has been held for metropolitan and district wise school. Applicants of class one to class nine can check their Non Govt School Lottery Result. The digital lottery has been held for 2907 non govt high schools of our country.

Congratulations to the students who have been selected in the Private School Admission Lottery Result merit list. A waiting list also been issued by DSHE. The lottery draw is live for all the students and parents. Then Non Government School Admission Result can be downloaded from gsateletalk com bd website. Applicants will need their user id to download their gsa result 2023non government online.
